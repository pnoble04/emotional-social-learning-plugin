Progress bar

Depending on the assignment settings, your progress bar takes into account those of the below that apply:

  • Whether you have started working on the assignment, i.e. created a new submission.
  • Whether you have finalized your work and submitted your assignment.
  • Whether you have evaluated your experience working with your teammates, if the assignment is done in teams and teammate evaluations are enabled.
  • Whether you have finished your first peer evaluation, if peer evaluations are enabled.
  • Whether you have finished 5 or more peer evaluations, if peer evaluations are enabled.
